1. Introduction

Shoopt is a mobile app that helps users optimize their grocery shopping by taking product photos and detecting store locations using GPS.

2. Collected Data

  • Product photos: processed by AI to extract product name and price.
  • GPS location: used to detect nearby stores via Google Maps API.
  • Authentication: Email/password or Google Sign-In.
  • Technical data: device info for app functionality.
  • Future analytics: planned integration of Firebase Analytics in production.

3. Data Storage

Beta version: Data stored locally on your device.

Production version: Cloud storage may be used in future updates.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect your data to improve your shopping experience and provide core app features.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data, except for:

  • Product recognition (Hugging Face)
  • Location services (Google Maps API)

6. Security

We implement technical security measures but no method is 100% secure.
